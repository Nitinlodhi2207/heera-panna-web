Heera Panna Saree – Business Research Brief

For Website Planning & Content Direction

1. Business Overview

Heera Panna Saree is a saree retail business located in Rajwada, Indore. They specialize mainly in:

Maheshwari sarees

Silk sarees

Premium traditional sarees

Occasion wear

Daily wear cotton sarees

The website must present the store as a trusted saree brand with a premium, heritage-oriented feel.

2. Business Goals for the Website

Build a strong online presence for the brand.

Showcase sarees digitally in a clean, premium catalog format.

Convert visitors into enquiries through:

WhatsApp button

Request Price button

Simple enquiry form

Improve brand trust — store location, history, product quality.

Use blogs and content to improve visibility over time (SEO-friendly articles).

The website is not for online payments — only for display + enquiries.

3. Target Customers (Buyer Personas)
a) Local Indore Customers (Main Audience)

Women 30–60

Looking for premium or wedding sarees

Prefer visiting the shop after seeing products online

Want quick WhatsApp enquiry option

b) Younger Shoppers / Gifting Buyers

Women 22–35

Browse online for inspiration

Prefer stylish visuals, nice photography

Want easy contact and quick response

c) Cultural & Traditional Buyers

Interested in authentic Maheshwari sarees

Want fabric details, handloom information

Trust stores with history and clarity

4. Brand Positioning

The website must reflect:

Heritage of Maheshwari sarees

Indore local trust (Rajwada location is a big advantage)

Premium product feel

Honest, clean presentation

Easy communication via WhatsApp

Tone should be:

Warm

Polite

Culturally rooted

Trustworthy

No aggressive marketing tone needed.

5. Visual Style & Design Direction
Overall Look

Clean

Light colours (cream, beige, gold, pastel tones)

Traditional + modern blend

High focus on product photography

Photography Requirements

For each product:

3 to 4 images minimum

Well-lit photos

Clear display of fabric and pallu

Close-ups of border/weave

A flat lay shot

Optional: draped shot on mannequin

Brand Elements Needed from Client

Logo (HD)

Color palette (if any)

Store images (optional)

Shopfront photos

Staff photo (for "About Us")

6. Product Information Requirements

For each saree:

Product Name

Fabric Type (Maheshwari/Silk/Cotton etc.)

Colour

Design/Pattern

Description (short)

Detailed description (longer)

What’s included (blouse piece etc.)

Occasion type

Weight (optional)

Availability (In Store / Available)

3–5 images

7. Collections / Categories Suggested

These are business-friendly categories:

Maheshwari Sarees

Silk & Semi-Silk Sarees

Cotton & Daily Wear

Wedding / Festive Sarees

Unique Picks / New Arrivals

Most Loved Sarees

This keeps catalog simple and familiar.

8. Essential Website Content
Homepage Must Contain

Hero banner (best saree images + location highlight)

Short intro about store

Highlight categories

“Request Price on WhatsApp” CTA

Featured sarees

New arrivals section

Customer trust blocks (e.g., 20+ years experience)

Store address & Google Map

About Us Page

Story of store

How long they’ve been serving customers

What they specialise in

Why they are a trusted saree shop

Photos of shop/owner if client is comfortable

Contact Page

Store address

WhatsApp button

Phone number

Contact form

Google Map embed

Store timings

Blog Ideas

To support ranking and get traffic:

“How to identify a Maheshwari saree”

“Best sarees for wedding functions in Indore”

“Maheshwari saree care and maintenance tips”

“Top saree colours for festive season”

(3–5 blogs per month as part of your social media plan.)

9. Content & Copy Tone for Website

Polite

Clear

Rooted in tradition

Focus on fabric, craftsmanship, colour, and trust

Avoid overselling

Short sentences, readable layout.

10. Requirements From Client (Before Starting)

Ask the client for:

50 saree images (3–5 images each)

Basic product descriptions

Store information (address, timings, contact number)

About Us story (short paragraph)

Any color preference or mood

Logo in PNG/SVG

WhatsApp number for enquiry

Any brand tagline (optional)

11. Internal Development Team Notes (Non-Technical)

Keep the website extremely simple to navigate

No complicated features

Catalog should load fast and display images cleanly

Request Price CTA must be visible on all product pages

Blog section should be easy for client to use

Product CMS fields must be simple and minimal

Contact and WhatsApp buttons must be prominent

12. Final Website Purpose Summary

This website should serve as a digital showroom, not an e-commerce platform.
It must:

Show products clearly

Build trust

Generate enquiries

Offer simple browsing

Support SEO through blogs

Represent the brand positively
